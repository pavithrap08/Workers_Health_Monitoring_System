Quick Safety Tips (micro-break + on-site)
1. Hydrate: small sips every 20–30 minutes (especially in heat).
2. Micro-break = reset: 3–5 minutes is enough to reduce fatigue and mistakes.
3. Breathing reset: inhale 4s → hold 2s → exhale 6s (repeat 5 times).
4. Posture check: relax shoulders, unclench jaw, loosen grip for 10 seconds.
5. Eye relief (if screen/work focus): 20 seconds, look 20 feet away.
6. Heat warning: if sweating + dizziness → stop, move to shade, inform supervisor.
7. Low SpO₂ / breathlessness: rest immediately and avoid exertion.
8. After a fall alert: stay still, call for help—don’t “walk it off”.
